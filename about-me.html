Hi, my name is Deeti and welcome to my blog page.

Through this blog page I will be documenting and describing my programming journey. I will review courseworks I am following, my struggles and insights. I am planning to regularly write about my feelings on learning on programming.

My on and off struggle to learn Programming

Though on papers I started to learn five years ago, it is only this year, I am finally got my teeth into the game. I started learning front end through Free Code Camp and after completing their second certificate, I moved to Scrimba to learn front end development.

One good learning so far

Before this year, I was learnig to programme through different platforms and courses. I kept pushing myself through knowing I wasn't learning. I wasn't working on personal projects and I was mostly doing follow along coding. I wasted a lot of time that way. However, when I switched to Free Code Camp and Scrimba, they quickly put off the training wheel and I slowly started working on solo projects from scratch. This made me realise how important it is to start writing asap when learning programming.
